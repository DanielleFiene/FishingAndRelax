Prices and Conditions

Prices 2025

  • Fish sales:
    • Carp (40–70 cm): €5 / 125 CZK per kilo.
    • Trout (minimum 30 cm): €7 / 175 CZK per kilo.

  • Fishing permits:
    • Day pass: €10 / 250 CZK per person (2 rods).
      Extra rod: +€2 / 50 CZK.
    • Weekend pass (Sat–Sun): €18 / 450 CZK per person.
      Extra rod: +€4 / 100 CZK.
    • Weekly pass: €72 / 1800 CZK per person (3 rods).

  • Accommodation prices and deposit:
    • Small tent (2 people): €15 / 375 CZK per night.
    • Large tent (2 people): €25 / 625 CZK per night.
    • Extra person: €5 / 125 CZK per night.
    • Cottage (4 people): €50 / 1250 CZK per night.
    • Cottage per week: €350 / 8750 CZK.
    • A deposit is required for renting the cottage. It will be refunded within 5 working days after inspection.
    • A deposit is required for renting a bicycle and/or fishing rod. It will be refunded within 5 working days after inspection.

Conditions

  • Season & Opening Hours:
    • Season: April to October.
    • Opening hours: daily from 08:00 to 20:00.

  • House Rules:
    • Check-in is possible until 20:00. Arrivals after this time only in consultation with the managers.
    • Check-out must be before 10:00 on the day of departure.
    • If you rent the cottage: please leave it swept clean upon departure.
    • Visitors are welcome but must report to the managers.
    • Children must always be supervised by an adult, especially near the water and fire pit.
    • Dogs are allowed provided they do not disturb other guests, animals, or nature. Droppings must be cleaned up immediately!
    • Use of glassware is not allowed on the piers or directly near the water.
    • The use of confetti, fireworks, or party tents is not allowed.
    • Do not leave valuables unattended. The organization is not responsible for theft or damage.
    • Please leave facilities clean for the next user.
    • The use of scooters, mopeds, or other motorized vehicles on the premises is not allowed without permission.
    • Do not leave trash or food waste behind, not even on the campsite or at fishing spots.
    • Loud music is not allowed, even if you are the only guest.
    • Open fire is only allowed at the designated fire pit.
    • Drones are only allowed with the permission of the managers.
    • In case of emergencies or nuisance: report immediately to the managers.
