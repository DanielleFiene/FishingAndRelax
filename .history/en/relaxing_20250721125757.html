Relax at our natural fishing ponds

Escape the hustle and enjoy pure tranquility surrounded by greenery and water. Our accommodations and relaxation spots are perfect for those who want to unwind, walk, paint, read, or simply enjoy the silence.

Make use of our cozy fire pit for an evening by the campfire, relax, or venture out into nature with your favorite book or a warm drink.

Our hosts speak Dutch, German, English, and Czech and are happy to help make your stay as pleasant as possible.

Open from April through October, daily from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Please note that only the cottage is equipped with electricity, generated by solar panels. For campers and tents, no electricity is available. Additionally, there is an outdoor toilet but no shower.

Relaxation Rules

  • You are in a nature reserve. Please respect the environment.
  • Playing loud music is not allowed, even if you are the only guest. This remains a unique natural area.
  • You may use the fire pit and smoke barrel free of charge.
  • Starting your own campfire is not permitted.
  • If camping, your spot will be assigned by the managers.
  • If you rent the cottage, please leave it broom-clean after your stay.
  • Report any breakage or damage to the managers. They will decide if compensation is required.
  • Please stay away from the electrical installation.
  • Do not leave food scraps behind.
  • Fish waste must be burned at the fire pit.
  • Park your car only in designated areas.
