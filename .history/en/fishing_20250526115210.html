Fishing for mirror carp & rainbow trout

Catch mirror carp and rainbow trout in our natural ponds in the Czech Republic. Choose from a day, weekend, or week pass. Online ordering is not possible; please contact us to reserve.

You may use your own fishing rod or rent one for a small extra fee. Barbless hooks are mandatory, and respect for nature and fish is essential. Catch & release is of course also allowed.

You can prepare your catch on site (extra charge per kilo/fish). Making a campfire and using the smoking barrel are possible for a true outdoor experience.

We are open from April through October, daily from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Fishing rules

  • You are in a nature reserve. Keep the environment clean.
  • Fishing is allowed with 1, 2 or 3 rods depending on your permit.
  • Always use barbless hooks.
  • With a casting rod, lures may have a maximum of 2 hooks.
  • Keep a minimum distance of 30 meters from other anglers when using a casting rod.
  • Mandatory equipment: unhooking mat, landing net, and hook remover.
  • Feeding is only allowed with boilies and pellets.
  • If you leave your fishing spot, please reel in your rods.
  • Minimum distance between anglers is 20 meters unless otherwise permitted by the managers.
  • The managers may prohibit feeding if necessary.
  • Carefully return caught fish and assist them, especially after a long fight, to swim away.
  • Damage to fish caused by careless handling must be compensated.
